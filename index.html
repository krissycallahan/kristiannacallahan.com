About Me

Kristianna Callahan is a pianist and teacher dedicated to helping students build confidence at the piano.

  • Over 10 years of piano teaching experience
  • Bachelor's degree in Piano Performance from the University of Utah
  • Co-creator of the music education apps BeesKeys and IntervalJump

Her teaching emphasizes strong technique, music literacy, and meaningful musical experiences at every level.

Lessons

Children

Piano lessons designed to be fun, interactive, and rewarding. Students learn note reading, rhythm, and essential piano skills through games and activities that reinforce learning while keeping them excited to come back each week.

Available in-home & online

Teens

Supportive instruction that meets teens where they are — whether pursuing classical repertoire, contemporary favorites, or preparing for auditions. Technique and theory are woven naturally into every lesson.

Available in-home & online

Adults

Adult lessons focus on getting you playing music you enjoy as quickly as possible. By learning chords, patterns, and practical piano skills from the start, you'll be able to play real songs while building a strong musical foundation—whether you're a beginner or returning after years away.

Available in-home & online
